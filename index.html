Our Story

First Meet

29 Feb 2020

John and Jane first met at a bustling coffee shop in the heart of the city. Both engrossed in their own worlds, they accidentally reached for the same book on a shelf. Their hands touched, and they looked into each other's eyes, sharing an instant connection. A conversation sparked, and from that moment, they knew their lives were about to change

First Date

29 Mar 2020

Their first date was a romantic dinner at a cozy Italian restaurant. The atmosphere was filled with laughter and shared stories. As they savored each bite of pasta, they discovered their shared love for travel and a passion for music. The evening ended with a stroll under the starry sky, where they held hands for the first time, feeling a strong bond growing between them.

Proposal

29 Mar 2023

Several years later, John proposed to Jane at the same coffee shop where they first met. He led her to the very shelf where they had touched hands for the first time. There, he got down on one knee and asked her to spend the rest of their lives together. Tears of joy welled up in her eyes as she said "yes," sealing their love story that began with an accidental touch and blossomed into a lifelong journey of love and adventure.
